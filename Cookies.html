  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Add 2-1/4 cups of flour, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1 teaspoon of baking soda in one bowl
  3. Beat 1 cup of butter, 3/4 of granulated sugar, 3/4 of light brown sugar, 2 eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract in another bowl
  4. Stir the two bowls together until well mixed and it becomes a dough
  5. Add 2 cups milk chocolate chips and 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips and mix well
  6. Bake at 375F for 20 minutes or until golden
